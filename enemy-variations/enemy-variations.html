Types of Enemies

Name: Dryvan, the star Scout
Species: Zephyrite (Atmospheric Drifter)
Homeworld: Gliese 1214 B

Originating from Gliese 1214 b, a planet engulfed in perpetual storms and vapor oceans, the Zephyrites evolved as semi-gaseous entities capable of traversing atmospheric currents with fluid precision. Designed for observation and infiltration, Dryvan was captured by Blorgath’s fleet while surveying the ruins of Kepler-22b. Impressed by its adaptability and sensory acuity, Blorgath integrated Dryvan into the Abyssal Armada as a scout and intelligence operative.

Name: GRIVOK, THE BIO-FORGE ENFORCER
Species: Vornid Brute
Homeworld: TRAPPIST-1 e

On TRAPPIST-1 e, life endured through strength and mutation. The planet’s acidic jungles and relentless volcanic activity forged the Vornids, a brutal species encased in organic armor. When the Abyssal Armada descended upon the world, Blorgath’s biomech engineers harvested Vornid genetic material to construct the ultimate ground enforcer—Grivok, the first of the Bio-Forges.

Name: PLYXA, THE HIVE HEALER
Species: Seraphel Symbiote
Homeworld: Kepler-452 b

The once-harmonious world of Kepler-452 b was home to the Seraphel, a luminous species bound through a global psychic network. Their civilization, rooted in empathy and resonance, was extinguished when the Abyssal Armada corrupted their telepathic system using biomechanical technology. The Seraphel’s unity was dismantled, their songs replaced by silence. From the remnants of this fallen race, Plyxa was reconstructed as the Armada’s Hive Healer—a living conduit responsible for maintaining the bio-network linking the Invader fleets. She sustains and regenerates their biomechanical forces, ensuring uninterrupted communication and cohesion during invasions. Though traces of her original consciousness persist, they serve only as faint echoes within a being now devoted entirely to the Armada’s dominion.

Name: BLORGATH, THE SHELL TYRANT
Species: Crustacean–Cyborg Fusion
Homeworld: Kepler-22b

On Kepler-22b, a world of crushing oceanic depths and perpetual twilight, the crustacean- like Xarcid species thrived beneath immense pressure. When alien terraformers invaded their world to harvest geothermal energy, one guardian—Blorgath—merged with biomechanical nanites to resist the assault. The fusion of organism and machine transformed him into an apex predator capable of channeling immense thermal power.
